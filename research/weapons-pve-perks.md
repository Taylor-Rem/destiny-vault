# PvE Weapon Perks — deep reference (endgame, mid-2026)

> Focused research for keep/dismantle rules, tuned to an **endgame-PvE ability-spam Warlock**.
> Priority order used throughout: **boss DPS / ad-clear  >  ability regen  >  handling / QoL**.
> As of July 2026 (post *Monument of Triumph*, Update 9.7.0, 2026-06-09). PvE only — no PvP perks.

## 3. Economy / enabler perks (Column 3) and how they feed payoffs

| Perk | Mechanic | Feeds best |
|------|----------|-----------|
| **Ambitious Assassin** | Reload overflows mag based on rapid pre-reload kills (up to ~+50%) | Chain Reaction, Voltshot, Incandescent — signature Forbearance enabler |
| **Subsistence** | Kills refill mag from reserves (SMG/AR ~17%) | Kill-triggered payoffs (Incandescent, Destabilizing). **Does NOT arm Voltshot** |
| **Overflow** | On ammo pickup, overfills mag up to ~2x | Chain Reaction on low-mag special GLs. **Not a "reload" → won't arm Voltshot** |
| **Clown Cartridge** | Reload randomly overfills (≥1, up to ~+50%) | Reload-gated payoffs (Voltshot, Chain Reaction) — fires on reload |
| **Demolitionist** | Kills → grenade energy; **grenade cast auto-reloads** | Voltshot & reload-gated payoffs; also an ability-loop enabler |
| **Feeding Frenzy** | Kills stack reload speed (up to ~+120%) | **Voltshot** — near-instant reload re-applies Jolt (IKELOS combo) |
| **Reconstruction** | Passively refills mag to 2x while stowed | Incandescent/Chain Reaction on traces/GLs; Bait and Switch swaps |
| **Envious Assassin** | Kills with *other* weapons overflow this mag up to ~3x | Chain Reaction burst on special GLs; boss one-mag phases |
| **Envious Arsenal** | *Damage* with both other weapons refills this one | Same as Assassin, easier trigger (newer) |
| **Lead from Gold** | Heavy ammo bricks reload this weapon | Rocket-sidearm Voltshot sustain (Indebted Kindness) |

> **Critical gotcha for rules:** Voltshot and other **"after reloading"** perks REQUIRE a real reload.
> Pair them with reload-refunders (Feeding Frenzy, Demolitionist, Clown Cartridge, Reconstruction) —
> **Subsistence/Overflow will NOT re-arm Voltshot.**

## 4. Ability-energy perks (secondary priority — nice, not required)

Your builds already feed abilities, so these are a *bonus*, not a keep-driver on their own:
- **Demolitionist** — grenade energy on kill + auto-reload on grenade cast (also a top enabler, §3).
- **Wellspring** — ability energy (grenade/melee/class, cycles) on kill.
- **Pugilist** — melee energy on kill.
- **Grave Robber** — melee kills reload; powered-melee reloads from stowed.
- **Osmosis** — matches weapon element to your grenade element after a grenade.
- **Shoot to Loot** — shoot ammo/orbs to collect (enables Orb/trace builds).
- **Heal Clip** — reload-after-kill heals you + allies (survivability, not energy).

## 5. Deprioritize — handling / QoL / awkward (low keep-value in PvE)

Treat as filler unless they're the *only* thing on an otherwise-desired weapon:
- **Handling/QoL:** Slickdraw, Quickdraw, Fluted/handling masterworks, Hip-Fire Grip, Elemental
  Capacitor (handling mode), Perpetual Motion (mild), Well-Rounded (mild).
- **PvP-oriented (irrelevant to your play):** Snapshot Sights, Moving Target, Opening Shot,
  Rangefinder, Eye of the Storm, Zen Moment, Killing Wind, Threat Detector, Under Pressure.
- **Awkward/conditional:** perks needing finicky setup for little PvE payoff (e.g. Successful Warm-Up
  needing full ability energy, Rewind Rounds' sustained-precision requirement on non-DPS guns).

## 6. Rarity / coverage (for the "preserve one source" rule)

⚠️ **Correction vs. common assumption:** being *strong* ≠ being *rare*. light.gg shows **"100+ weapons"**
can roll **Bait and Switch** and **Precision Instrument** — they are **widespread**, so they should
**NOT** trigger a "last copy" preservation rule despite being top-tier. Same for Vorpal, Frenzy,
Incandescent, Voltshot, Chain Reaction (common on GLs), Golden Tricorn, Reconstruction.

**⚠️ How to actually measure rarity (verified 2026-07-01 via light.gg in-browser):** on any perk's
page `light.gg/db/items/<id>/<slug>/`, the number that matters is **"Randomly Rolls On (N)"** — NOT the
much smaller "Craftable On" / "Curated Roll On" counts. Earlier research conflated those and badly
overstated rarity. Verified counts:

| Perk | Randomly Rolls On | Rarity |
|------|-------------------|--------|
| **Recombination** | **10** | ✅ genuinely rare — preserve a source |
| **Chill Clip** | 46 | uncommon; unique freeze/shield-strip → still worth a source |
| Firing Line | 65 | common-ish (**Taipan-4fr can roll it**) |
| Jolting Feedback | 92 | common |
| Successful Warm-Up | 99 | common |
| Envious Arsenal | 100+ | common (the "~7" everyone cites is the *craftable* count) |
| Cascade Point | 100+ | common |
| Bait and Switch, Precision Instrument, Envious Assassin, Reconstruction | 100+ | common |

So the only **globally rare** trait verified here is **Recombination (10)**; Chill Clip (46) is a
distant second worth keeping for its unique freeze. Archetype-locked new verbs (**Detonator Beam** =
traces, **Bolt-Charge** perks, **Thermal Atomization** = Blasters, **Chaos Reshaped** = Salvation's Edge)
are limited *by availability*, not roll-count — verify each on light.gg before hard-coding.

> **Better rule for THIS vault (what the user actually asked for):** "preserve one source" is best
> computed **relative to the user's own inventory**, not global rarity — i.e. *if only one weapon in the
> vault has perk X, keep it*, regardless of how common X is globally. That's directly computable from the
> sockets data and is what the classifier should implement. Global rarity is a tiebreaker/nice-to-have.

> **Exotics:** unique by nature — keep exactly one of each; extras of the same exotic are safe dismantles
> unless the tier/roll differs meaningfully.

## 7. Balance changes that affect YOUR play (ability-spam, super-in-rotation)
- ⚠️ **Monument of Triumph cut Super energy gained from boss damage by ~60%.** Supers now come back
  much slower *during* damage phases — so a Chaos Reach / Nova "super every phase" rotation is weaker
  than it used to be. Your Alpha build's ionic-trace super refund partly offsets this, but lean on
  **weapon** boss-DPS (Taipan, a rocket/LFR) as the backbone rather than expecting a super each phase.
- Ability cooldowns cut ~20% globally, but the active-cooldown cap dropped 190% → 125% (less value from
  stacking cooldown sources).
- **Fusion rifles buffed** (Renegades): High-Impact PvE scalar 6.7% → 15% — relevant if you want a
  boss-DPS special beyond Taipan.
- Rocket Pulse Rifles nerfed; primaries buffed +30% vs minors (+40% exotic) — good for ad-clear primaries.
