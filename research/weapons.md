## A. Weapon perks & god rolls

**As of 2026-07-01** (post *Monument of Triumph*, Update 9.7.0, June 9 2026 — Destiny 2's final
major live-service update; sandbox effectively frozen).

### Roll structure
Modern legendary roll sockets: Column 1 (barrels/blades/bowstrings/scopes), Column 2
(magazines/arrows/batteries/guards), Column 3 (Trait 1), Column 4 (Trait 2), Origin Trait (since
Witch Queen S16), Masterwork, Weapon Mod. The Col-3 × Col-4 pairing defines the god roll.

**Weapon Tier system (1–5):** introduced in Edge of Fate, expanded in Monument of Triumph. Weapons
drop at a tier and upgrade via the upgrade-mod socket (Glimmer + Ascendant Alloys + Enhancement
Prisms). T1 baseline → T2 ~two pre-enhanced perks → T3 first meaningful threshold (enhanced perks in
columns) → T4 enhanced barrels/mags/mods → T5 ceiling (up to 3 enhanced perks/column, enhanced origin
trait, ornament, Combat Flair mod slot). Sources:
- Shattered Vault — Weapon Perks: https://shatteredvault.com/kb/weapon-optimization/weapon-perks/
- Shattered Vault — Tiers: https://shatteredvault.com/kb/weapon-optimization/weapon-tiers/
- Bungie Dev Insights (Weapons/Artifacts/Focusing): https://www.bungie.net/7/en/News/article/dev_insights_weapons_artifacts_focusing_preview
- D2 Wiki — Weapon Perks: https://d2.destinygamewiki.com/wiki/Weapon_Perks

**Golden Tricorn**, **Frenzy**. Economy: **Reconstruction** (double-mag while stowed), **Envious

(Void overshield), **Attrition Orbs** (Orbs from sustained dmg, Renegades), **Onslaught** (Renegades).

Enhancement folded into the Tier 1–5 track; weapons drop pre-enhanced by tier; upgrade via mod socket
rather than per-perk cores. Monument of Triumph: crafted legendaries eligible for tier upgrades without
re-farming, unlocked past-season crafted weapons, added ~25 new catalysts + 9 catalyst perks. Sources:
- Shattered Vault — Enhancing: https://shatteredvault.com/kb/weapon-optimization/weapon-enhancements/

## B. Weapon archetypes & meta (mid-2026)

**Final update:** Monument of Triumph (Update 9.7.0, 2026-06-09). Timeline: Edge of Fate (mid-2025,
Gear Tiers + deterministic ammo economy) → Renegades (~2025-12-02, ammo refinements, Orders, exotic
reworks) → Monument of Triumph (primary/special buffs, catalyst pass, auto rifles → 400 RPM).

### Slots & ammo
Three-slot structure (Kinetic/Energy/Power) unchanged; ammo type (Primary/Special/Heavy) gates
loadouts. **Deterministic ammo economy:** Special/Heavy meters fill from kills → guaranteed brick;
scales with new Ammo Generation stat. Renegades tightened it (~17% less meter from minors; Crucible
special bricks persist 60s). Sources:
- Guild Order wiki — archetypes/slots: https://guildorder.com/games/destiny2/wiki/weapon-archetypes
- GamesRadar gear tiers: https://www.gamesradar.com/games/destiny/destiny-2-gear-tiers/
- Shacknews Edge of Fate changes: https://www.shacknews.com/article/144881/changes-in-destiny-2-the-edge-of-fate
- Destiny Bulletin Renegades ammo economy: https://destinybulletin.com/news/bungie-kicks-off-destiny-2-renegades-sandbox-previews-with-hud-tweaks-new-gear-changes-and-an-ammo-economy-overhaul/

### Damage types
Six: Kinetic, Solar, Arc, Void, Stasis, Strand. Matching shield color = 200% dmg. Prismatic is a
subclass, not a weapon element. Sources:
- Destinypedia elemental damage: https://www.destinypedia.com/Elemental_damage
- D2 Wiki elements: https://d2.destinygamewiki.com/wiki/Elements

### Sandbox changes affecting keep-value
Primary +30–40% vs minors; Special +20% vs elites (excl. snipers/rocket pulses/rocket sidearms/swords/
Choir of One); special ammo tightened; Heavy Burst Slug Shotguns buffed; Rocket Pulse Rifles nerfed;
Auto Rifles → 400 RPM; every exotic now has a catalyst; exotic primaries +40% vs minors. Sunsetting is
dead (legacy gear usable at cap) BUT dismantled weapons are unrecoverable. Sources:
- Bungie Renegades weapon tuning: https://www.bungie.net/7/en/News/Article/dev_insight_renegades_weapons
